PUMA LQDCELL AR App

PUMA’s latest innovation, LQDCELL technology, draws on the footwear brand’s roots. INPHANTRY was tasked with introducing physical sneakers through a digital lens.

We began with a survey of camera-centric apps to understand how best to serve functionality.

An iterative process meant going back to the drawing board to figure out how we might best explain what was happening and how to help new shoe owners utilize the filters.

Working on an app that relies on machine learning was definitely a leap for me. Constant contact with our development team was necessary to understand and update one another on progress and troubleshooting.

The app is built to support future drops with new AR content.
