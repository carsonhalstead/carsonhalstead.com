Milestones of a Steel Giant

Boston's Nubian Square (formerly Dudley Square) MBTA bus depot features an original work of architecture from the era of an elevated Orange Line train.

The addition of unobtrusive banners help daily commuters learn more about an easily overlooked aspect of the development of public transportation.

Each individual banner reads like a newspaper headline.

Accompanied by a date, the text offers perspectives from the current state of public transportation within the city.

The purpose and history of the project is laid out in a handbound proposal with images of in situ installations.
