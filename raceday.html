NSAF Race Day App

Supporting a nationwide athletic event with a digital companion.

Year after year, talented high school athletes from across the country gather at the New Balance Nationals Outdoor track meet.

The RaceDay app provides a system to support the meet by giving participants, attendees and coaches a dedicated platform for tracking events. Follow athlete profiles, check in on your teammates, and stay updated on results in real time.

I developed a style guide to establish a consistent visual language throughout the interface.
