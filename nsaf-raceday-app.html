NSAF Race Day App

Year after year, talented high school athletes from across the country gather at the New Balance Nationals Outdoor track meet. The 2017 meet offered the opportunity to put relevant information in the hands of coaches, competitors, and fans alike. The RaceDay app provides a system to support the meet and influenced the experience of events to come.

Follow athlete profiles, check in on your teammates, and stay updated on results regardless of your location.

I developed a style guide to establish a consistent visual language throughout the interface.

Download the app for iOS and Android
